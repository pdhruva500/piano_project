What ages do you teach?

I teach students of all ages, from young beginners to adults.

Do I need a piano to start lessons?

Ideally, yes. A keyboard with weighted keys is acceptable for beginners, but an acoustic or digital piano is recommended for optimal learning.

How long are lessons?

Lesson lengths vary based on the student's age and level. Typical lessons range from 30 to 60 minutes.

Do you teach online or in-person?

Please contact me to discuss the current options available for lessons.

What style of music do you teach?

I teach a broad range of musical styles and adapt to each student's interests and goals. My approach combines reading music with playing by ear.
